This chart shows us the number of movie genre between 1950 - 2016, and absolute counts and percentage of releases, and also total number of movies in 1950-2016 years.


This chart highlights short and middle length movies are watched with high percentage. This is because of the high number of movies produced in these two lengths. One of the reason is it is easier to reach and share short movies on social media and other electronic platforms. The other reason is people have the chance to watch it at their break times.
